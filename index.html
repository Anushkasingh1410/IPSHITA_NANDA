Professor & Academic Leader

IILM University, Greater Noida

Prof. (Dr) Ipseeta Nanda is an accomplished academic leader specializing in System-on-Chip Design for IoT, Artificial Intelligence, and Machine Learning. With a distinguished career spanning over 19 years, she has made significant contributions to both academia and research.

Academic Credentials

BTech, MTech, MBA (HR & Data Analytics), PhD (BPUT, State Govt. University), and Post Doctoral Fellow with research experience at Asian Institute of Technology, Bangkok LInCOLN University College, Malaysia.

Currently serving as Professor at IILM University and NEP-2020 coordinator. Previously established the Faculty of Information Technology & Engineering at Gopal Narayan Singh University as its Founding Dean. Her expertise in Outcome-based Education (OBE) has helped numerous institutions in NAAC and NBA accreditation processes.

Research Excellence

Recipient of multiple Best Paper Awards internationally, including honors in Kuala Lumpur and Pune, with recent recognition at Garden College University, Bangalore, as well as in Vietnam in an International conference as Best Presenter
